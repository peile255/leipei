About

Lei Pei is an earth scientist passionate about deciphering the patterns hidden within geologic archives and satellite observations. With a PhD in Structural Geology from the University of Science and Technology, Lei bridges quantitative analysis and field-based insights to address tectonic evolution, resource sustainability, and natural hazard resilience.

Lei currently works as a research scientist at the Center for Geospatial Innovation, leading initiatives that apply AI-driven remote sensing to monitor landscape change, critical mineral deposits, and infrastructure risk.
