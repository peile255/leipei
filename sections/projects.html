Flagship Initiatives

A snapshot of collaborative projects that blend geoscience, remote sensing, and data storytelling to support decision-makers.

Atlas of Active Faults

Building a living atlas of tectonic structures integrating InSAR, paleoseismic trenches, and GNSS velocities to guide seismic hazard planning.

MineWatch

A monitoring system that measures the footprint of mining activities in near-real time, providing transparency for governments and investors.

IcePulse

Partnering with Arctic communities to co-design satellite-based early warning and adaptation strategies for thaw-related impacts.

GeoInsights Studio

Producing interactive narratives and visualizations that translate complex geoscience data into actionable stories for policymakers.
