Continental Deformation

This theme explores how continental crust accommodates tectonic stress across plate boundaries and intraplate regions. By combining field mapping with satellite geodesy, I seek to quantify deformation rates and illuminate subsurface structures.

Bayan Har Fault System Dynamics

Multi-temporal InSAR analysis reveals strain partitioning across the eastern Tibetan Plateau, constraining seismic hazard models for densely populated basins.

3D Structural Restoration

Balanced cross sections and stratigraphic modeling to reconstruct the evolution of thrust belts over millions of years.

Crustal Rheology Experiments

Laboratory deformation of rock analogs paired with numerical modeling to evaluate rheological parameters that best fit observed strain patterns.
